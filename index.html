Training Safety Basics
  • Always warm up for 5 minutes before working out — cold muscles are more injury-prone.
  • Prioritize form over speed or weight. A slower, clean rep beats a fast, sloppy one.
  • Stop a set if you feel sharp or joint pain (different from normal muscle fatigue/burn).
  • Rest at least one full day between hard sessions for the same muscle group.
  • Progress gradually — add reps, sets, or a small amount of weight only once current reps feel easy with good form.
  • Stay hydrated, and eat protein and carbs within a couple hours of training to support recovery.
  • Sleep is when most muscle recovery happens — aim for 7-9 hours nightly.
  • This app provides general fitness education, not medical advice. Check with a doctor before starting a new program, especially with any existing injury or condition.

Install on Your Phone

iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen, then choose "Add to Home Screen". This installs Daily Gym as a full-screen app icon, no App Store needed.

Android (Chrome): Tap the ⋮ menu in the top right, then choose "Add to Home screen" or "Install app". You may also see an automatic install banner.

Once installed, the app works offline — your workout plan and progress are stored right on your phone.
